Modeling of a Water Treatment and Distribution Network Using AutoCAD Plant 3D

Project Description

Design and 3D modeling of an industrial water treatment and circulation network using AutoCAD Plant 3D.

The system consists of the following components:

  • Water storage tank
  • Treatment filters
  • Heat exchanger
  • Circulation pumps
  • Interconnected piping network
