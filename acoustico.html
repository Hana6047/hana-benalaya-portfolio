Acoustico CNC Machine

Acoustic Foam Cutting – Engineering Project

Project Description

Acoustico is a CNC machine designed for precise cutting of acoustic foam. The project combines mechanical design, automation, and embedded systems.

Technical Details

  • Mechanical design & simulation: SolidWorks
  • CNC control firmware: GRBL
  • Microcontroller: ESP32
  • Toolpath generation: G-code
  • Testing: Universal G-code Sender
